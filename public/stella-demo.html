The top of the funnel is leaking

Families used to begin on Google, where APFM owned the page. Now they begin by asking — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, AI Overviews. Those engines can describe senior care, but they can't see live availability, real pricing, or book a tour. APFM's demand starts where APFM is no longer present. Two moves plug the leak.

Move A · Get cited

GEO — Generative Engine Optimization

Be the source the answer engines quote. Structure APFM's 20 years of outcomes so ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity and AI Overviews cite APFM by name — the new "rank #1," measured as share of the answer.

Move B · Get transactable

An MCP for the marketplace

Being cited isn't enough — the assistant has to act. An MCP server lets any AI navigate APFM's marketplace and transact: search live availability, pull real pricing, book a tour, reserve a bed. The answer engine owns the question; APFM owns the transaction.

The building is a black box

Capturing demand is worthless if you route it to a community that can't take it. Today the supply side has no real-time capacity visualization — so referrals land on full buildings, open units sit dark, and no one can forecast the revenue bleeding out. This is the half we built: Stella's capacity visualization engine.

The unlock

One fuels the other

Demand intelligence and supply intelligence aren't two products — they're one loop. Every turn makes the next turn stronger.

Step 1 · Demand
Capture the question
GEO + the MCP turn every AI assistant into an APFM channel — families are captured where they now start.
Step 2 · Match
Route to real capacity
Stella matches that demand only to units a community can actually fill — no wasted referrals.
The Stella flywheel
Step 4 · Data
Publish live capacity
Filled buildings keep their real availability current — the freshest supply signal in senior care.
Step 3 · Fill
Fill the right units
Providers convert matched demand, occupancy climbs, and trust in APFM deepens.
Why it compounds: Richer live capacity data makes the MCP's answers more accurate and more bookable — so APFM wins more AI-origin demand on the very next query. The loop tightens with every placement.
The ask

Fund the loop, not the leak.

Patch the demand leak with GEO and an MCP, and give the supply side eyes with Stella's capacity engine. Neither survives alone — together they compound. Every family we route to a unit that's genuinely ready makes the next AI answer smarter, and the next family easier to serve.
